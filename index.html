About Us

The Candy Town Stalls

The Candy Town provides professionally managed live food stall services for weddings, corporate events, and large public gatherings. Our focus is on operational reliability, quality control, and smooth execution across events of all sizes.

We have successfully served over 30,000 guests in a single day and have supported high-footfall events including Nykaaland and India vs Pakistan match-day celebrations, reflecting our ability to manage scale without disruption.

The Candy Town is built on Trust, Experience, and Execution.
